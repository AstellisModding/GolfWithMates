# Golf With Mates

**Minecraft 1.21.1 · NeoForge**

A golf mod built for multiplayer. Place a course, grab a putter, and sink the ball — full shot physics, animated ball flight, and hole detection all included.

> Early alpha. Things will change, break, and get better.

## What's in the mod

### Blocks
| Block | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Golf Ball | Place and hit with a club. Animates along the simulated path to its rest position. |
| Golf Cup | The target. Ball automatically drops in when it rolls over one. |
| Golf Hole | Decorative hole marker for your course. |
| Golf Flag & Pole | Flag your holes so players know where to aim. |
| Golf Greenzone | Dedicated putting surface — low friction, true roll. |
| Nameplate | Label your holes or courses. |

### Items
| Item | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Putter | Right-click a golf ball to take a shot. Scroll while holding Shift to adjust power. |
| Poles Bag | Used in course construction. |
| Cup of Joe | A snack. Appa loves it. |

### Creatures
| Creature | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Appa | A friendly course companion. Tempt with a Cup of Joe. |

## How to play

1. Place a **Golf Ball** block on the ground
2. Hold your **Putter** and right-click the ball to take a shot
3. Use **Scroll + Shift** to adjust shot power before swinging
4. Watch the ball animate along its path to rest
5. Keep hitting until it rolls into a **Golf Cup**

The ball tracks your name and putt count — build a full course and compare scores with your mates.

## Installation

1. Install [NeoForge for 1.21.1](https://neoforged.net/)
2. Drop the mod `.jar` into your `mods/` folder
3. Launch and play
